Biography Experience Article Publications

  • Birth:4 September 1927, Boston, Massachusetts

  • Education:BS mathematics, California Institute of Technology (1948); PhD mathematics, Princeton University (1951).
  • Additional Info.:McCarthy considered himself an atheist.From 1978 to 1986, McCarthy developed the circumscription method of non-monotonic reasoning.McCarthy was a serious book reader, an optimist, and a staunch supporter of free speech.
